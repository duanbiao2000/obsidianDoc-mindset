你这张图里提到的几种 “Formula” —— **FRAI、QRS、HKABS、GMPAW** —— 是香港 DSE 英文写作教学常用的**结构模板体系**，用于不同文体（如社论、文章、议论文、演讲稿）快速组织段落与逻辑。  
这些缩写都是**写作框架记忆法（Writing Structure Formula）**，帮助学生在考试中保证完整、连贯、清晰的论证结构。

下面是逐一讲解与应用场景。

---

## 1️⃣ FRAI Formula — _Letter to the Editor / Letter Writing_

**用途：** 处理正式信件类题目，尤其是 _Letter to the Editor_（致编辑信）。  
**核心结构：**

|段落|含义|功能说明|
|---|---|---|
|**F – Format / Greeting**|开头格式与问候语|如 “Dear Editor,” 并表明来信目的。|
|**R – Reason for Writing**|写信原因|开宗明义说明为何写信（回应文章、表达意见等）。|
|**A – Arguments**|论点主体|每段一个清晰论点 + 支持例子或解释。|
|**I – Invitation / Closing**|结尾呼吁与感谢|通常包含呼吁读者行动或期望回应，例如 “I sincerely hope your readers will…”。|

**示例：**

> _Dear Editor,_  
> I am writing to express my concern about the growing problem of waste in our city…  
> _(F + R)_  
> To begin with, many citizens fail to realise the impact of their daily habits… _(A)_  
> I sincerely hope that more people will take recycling seriously. _(I)_

---

## 2️⃣ QRS Formula — _Article / Blog_

**用途：** 通用文章或博客文体，注重吸引读者与结构流畅。  
**结构：**

|段落|含义|功能说明|
|---|---|---|
|**Q – Question / Quote / Quick Hook**|吸引开头|以问题、引用或趣味句开场引起兴趣。|
|**R – Reasons / Reflections**|主体分析|逐点解释主题背后的原因或观点。|
|**S – Solution / Suggestion / Summary**|收尾|提出解决方案或总结个人见解。|

**示例：**

> _Have you ever wondered why students today are always stressed out?_ (Q)  
> Many of us are trapped by unrealistic expectations from society and ourselves. (R)  
> Perhaps it’s time to redefine success and embrace balance. (S)

---

## 3️⃣ HKABS Formula — _Argumentative / General Essay_

**用途：** 最常用于 **DSE Paper 2 长文论说文（Argumentative Essay）**。  
**结构逻辑：**

|段落|含义|功能说明|
|---|---|---|
|**H – Hook**|吸引引入|用名言、数据、问题引出主题。|
|**K – Key Idea / Thesis**|主题立场句|明确表达观点。|
|**A – Argument 1**|主论点一|逻辑论据 + 例子。|
|**B – Argument 2 / Body 2**|主论点二|进一步支持立场。|
|**S – Solution / Summary / Stand**|结论|总结并升华立场。|

**示例：**

> _In an age dominated by technology, should traditional classrooms disappear?_ (H)  
> I strongly believe that physical schools remain essential for social learning. (K)  
> Firstly, technology can never replace face-to-face human interaction. (A)  
> Moreover, schools teach discipline and teamwork—skills screens cannot provide. (B)  
> Education should embrace digital tools without abandoning the human touch. (S)

---

## 4️⃣ GMPAW Formula — _Speech Writing_

**用途：** 演讲稿题型，强调现场感、情感与号召力。  
**结构：**

|段落|含义|功能说明|
|---|---|---|
|**G – Greeting**|开场问候|如 “Good morning, teachers and fellow students.”|
|**M – Message / Main Idea**|明确主题|简述演讲目的或主要议题。|
|**P – Points / Persuasive Arguments**|主要论点|每点配合例子、修辞、比喻增强感染力。|
|**A – Appeal to Audience**|呼吁听众|直接面向听众发出邀请或挑战。|
|**W – Wrap-up / Ending**|收尾总结|用一句 memorable quote 或反问结尾。|

**示例：**

> _Good morning, everyone._ (G)  
> Today, I want to talk about why mental health matters as much as grades. (M)  
> Many of us feel overwhelmed by pressure, yet rarely talk about it. (P)  
> Let’s start supporting one another instead of competing blindly. (A)  
> Remember: grades fade, but kindness lasts. Thank you. (W)

---

## 总结：四大公式对照表

|模板|适用文体|逻辑方向|写作重点|
|---|---|---|---|
|**FRAI**|Letter to the Editor|正式沟通 → 表达意见|礼貌、清晰、结构完整|
|**QRS**|Article / Blog|吸引读者 → 分析 → 结语|语气自然、节奏轻快|
|**HKABS**|Argumentative Essay|立场明确 → 逻辑支撑 → 总结升华|理性、层次分明|
|**GMPAW**|Speech|开场 → 论点 → 呼吁 → 收尾|感情真挚、互动性强|
